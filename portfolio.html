04 — Practical Tip
If you've read the same page three times and still don't get it — the problem isn't you. It's probably the language.
Here's a study hack that actually works for students in India: When you're stuck on a concept, stop trying to understand it in English first. 1. Read the topic once in English (just to get the words) 2. Now explain it out loud to yourself in your mother tongue 3. If you can't explain it in your own language, you haven't understood it yet 4. Only then go back and match it with the English terms This is the Feynman Technique — desi style. Your brain processes things faster in the language it grew up thinking in. Use that. Don't fight it. We use this exact principle in every LearnKaro course. Concept first, language second. Save this. Share it with one batchmate who's struggling right now.
